About me

From a young age, I've always been intrigued by nature and the world around me, whether it's from a mathematical perspective or simply admiring the beautiful scenes it creates. I've wanted to find unique ways to capture and express my experiences. I love exploring different mediums, as art takes me into another world where I can find inner peace. I am passionate about it, and now that I have a solid foundation in art, I want to combine various cultures in my work and in the future, I want to address global issues trough my art. I believe that every little bit makes a difference, and I hope you can see my passion reflected in these pieces, each with its own story behind it.
